Dan Possumato and friends playing Irish, Scottish, French music

Abby and I checked out a musical performance at the Carnegie Library of Pittsburgh, main branch. They have a regular Sunday afternoon “World Kaleidoscope” series featuring world sounds from local musicians: two months ago I reported on some delightful local Appalachian music.

Today’s performance was by Dan Possumato and friends, featuring mostly Irish music, with Dan on button accordion.

We had a good time. Among the selections:

  • Scottish music
  • Irish jigs and reels
  • Irish waltz “Planxty Irwin” that Abby and I danced to
  • a French hanter dro
